◆ go_pose_dh_convert()

int go_pose_dh_convert ( const go_pose *  pose,
go_dh *  dh 
)

Converts pose to the equivalent DH parameters, stored in dh. Warning! Conversion from these DH parameters back to a pose via go_dh_pose_convert will NOT in general result in the same pose. Poses have 6 degrees of freedom, DH parameters have 4, and conversion to DH parameters loses some information. The source of this information loss is the convention imposed on DH parameters for choice of X-Y-Z axis directions. With poses, there is no such convention, and poses are thus freer than DH parameters.
