258 that accidental confusion of one type with another (which ought
259 to be diagnosed by the type system) can be diagnosed as unexpected
260 values. Note how HAL_RW is an exception to the powers-of-two rule,
261 as it is the bitwise OR of HAL_RO and the (nonexistent and nonsensical)
262 HAL_WO param direction.
263 */
